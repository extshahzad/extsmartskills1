Yes, 100%. Both courses are designed for complete beginners. You don't need any prior ecommerce, tech or business experience. We start from absolute zero.

The Video Series (PKR 999) is a pre-recorded 3-hour crash course you can watch at your own pace. The LIVE Class (PKR 1,999) is an interactive session where you can ask questions in real-time and get instant answers. Both come with an optional 1-year WhatsApp support upgrade.

With WhatsApp support (available for PKR 1,499 on Video Series and PKR 2,999 on LIVE Class), you get 1 full year of direct chat access to your trainer. Get help whenever you face a problem – whether it's a listing issue, account question, or scaling advice.
